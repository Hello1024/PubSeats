How to Use

Open the app and the locations of nearby pubs will be displayed.

Red pubs are at full capacity

Orange pubs are 1/3 to 2/3 full

Green are up to 1/3 full

The fullness is based on the previous weeks busy-ness (so 100% full means it is the highest it has been in the last week).

Please bear in mind this is a work in progress, any suggestions are always welcome on how to refine, as are feature requests.
